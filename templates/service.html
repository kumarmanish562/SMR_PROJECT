Our Specialization

As the name of our company, Bhasha Bharati, suggests, we specialize in the ‘Bhasha’ of ‘Bharat’ for Translation, Localization, and Interpretation.

Whether it is document translation or translation of technical, medical, or marketing subject, we have domain experts to ensure top quality translation.

We offer our professional translation services into English and all Indian Languages at highly competitive rates and in some cases, at less than 50% of the prevailing market rates in the USA, Canada, UK, and Europe.

This is possible because of:

  • Abundant availability of translator resources with us
  • Lower operating costs, as we are based in India
  • Availability of skilled professionals
  • Use of the latest CAT tools

Some of the clients who have retained us as their language partners include Bosch, TATA, Reliance, Netflix, Pfizer, to name a few.

Due to our strong network of professional translators with industry expertise, our internal quality processes, and the use of translation technology tools, we are able to achieve translation at a highly reduced cost to us. This enables us to offer better rates than the prevalent US and European market without sacrificing the quality of translation in any manner.

Bhasha Bharati and Shakti Enterprise are ISO 9001:2008 and DIN EN 1508 certified for quality standards. We are also members of ATA (American Translators Association) and ITA (Indian Translators Association).

We are registered as a vendor for providing Translation Services for most of the UN Organizations.
