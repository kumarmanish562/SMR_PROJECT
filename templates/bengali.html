Bengali Language Translation Services

Bengali is the native language of the Bengal region, which comprises the current nations of Bangladesh and parts of India, including West Bengal, Tripura, and southern Assam. With over 220 million native speakers, Bengali is the seventh most spoken language in the world and the second most spoken language in India.

Bengali is spoken not only in the Indian subcontinent but also by significant communities in the Middle East, Japan, United States, Singapore, Malaysia, and other countries.
