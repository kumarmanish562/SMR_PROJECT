Language Differs. But Translation Unites.

Kannada is a Dravidian language spoken predominantly in Karnataka. It is one of the scheduled languages of India and the official and administrative language of the state of Karnataka. There are approximately 40 million native speakers of Kannada in Karnataka, Kasaragod (Kerala), Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, and Andhra Pradesh. Kannada speakers can also be found in countries like the USA, Canada, Australia, Malaysia, Singapore, UK, Germany, Hong Kong, Mauritius, New Zealand, United Arab Emirates, and Thailand.

Kannada is written in the Kannada script, which evolved from the 5th-century Kadamba script.

Other Important Facts about Kannada Language:

  • ✅ Kannada is one of the oldest languages in the world with a rich literary tradition.
  • ✅ Kannada is the official language of Karnataka and is widely used in administration, education, and business.
  • ✅ Kannada is spoken by millions of people in India and globally in Kannada diaspora communities.
  • ✅ It has a strong influence in arts, literature, and culture.
