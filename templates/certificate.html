Our Specialization

Growing globally is the dream of many businesses, which is why the need for translating certificates is increasing at a rapid pace. Many documents like death certificates, birth certificates, passports, etc., have a global outreach. To make these documents readable by the concerned officials beyond borders, individuals or enterprises turn to translation services. Many organizations accept only those translated documents that contain a Certificate of Accuracy. This highlights the importance of our Certificate translation service.

Translation is needed to eliminate language barriers. Whether you are applying for a legal document, setting up your new business, or performing any such task, you will need to enclose your identity certificates. This is where our certificate translation service comes into the picture. Mere translation of a document does not guarantee that it would be used for official purposes. Hence, certification becomes necessary, as it creates a legal record.

BBA is one of the leading translation service providers in Mumbai, India, with over 5 decades of experience. Since our inception in 1968, we have been offering translation services accompanied by a certificate of accuracy. The projects we handle are assigned to native professional translators who understand all the language, translation, and cultural nuances. This makes the translated file valid and acceptable by foreign authorities. Whether it is a company or an individual, we have always provided cost-effective translations within specified deadlines.

We provide certified translations for the following:

  • Marriage certificate, divorce certificates, birth certificates, death certificates
  • Driver’s licenses, citizenship cards, passports, ration cards, visa copies, residence permits
  • Bank statements, tax receipts, TDS certificates
  • Academic degrees, diplomas, course certificates
  • Land-related documents
  • Wills, sale deeds, partnership deeds
  • Custom documents

Certified translations from us are accepted in:

  • Government offices
  • Schools, Colleges, and other educational institutions
  • Embassies/consulates for visas and residence permits
