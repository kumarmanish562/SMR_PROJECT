Punjabi Differs. But Translation Unites.

Punjabi is an Indo-Aryan language spoken by 102 million native speakers worldwide, making it the 10th most widely spoken language in the world. Punjabi is the fourth most spoken language in England and Wales and third most spoken in Canada. It also has a significant presence in the United Arab Emirates, USA, Saudi Arabia, and Australia.

Punjabi is written in both Devnagari and Gurmukhi scripts. It is an official language in Punjab, Haryana, and Delhi, and is a secondary officially recognized language in Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Rajasthan, and West Bengal.
