Gujarati Language Translation Services

Gujarati is an Indo-Aryan language native to the state of Gujarat. It is the official language of Gujarat and is also an official language in the Union territories of Daman and Diu, and Dadra and Nagar Haveli islands. There are about 54 million native speakers of Gujarati in India, and approximately 65 million speakers worldwide. Significant Gujarati-speaking populations can be found in the USA, Canada (especially in New York City and Greater Toronto), and the UK, where around 200,000 people speak the language.
