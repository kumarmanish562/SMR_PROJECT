Our Specialization

We have been providing, for over 50 years, professional legal translation from English to all Indian languages to local companies, global companies, law firms, and individuals in the language of jurisdiction.

Our long-standing commitment to quality, service, on-time delivery, and fair pricing policy has made us one of the most trusted translation agencies in India.

We are the certified agency for Legal Translation. Legal translation is a particular field requiring complete precision, knowledge of legal terms, comprehension of government controls, predominant laws, and the legal framework. At Bhasha Bharati, we understand the implications of a single misinterpretation of a legal term and the harm it can cause. We go to great lengths to select the best legal experts who are also in-country professional legal interpreters to do justice to the translation task.

The services that we provide in legal translation include patents, agreements, contracts, licenses, trademarks & copyrights, application & insurance forms, and many more.

We do justice to the translation task. We understand the importance of confidentiality, accuracy, and deadlines for legal translation. That’s why we have a project management team and qualified approved translators who specialize in:
