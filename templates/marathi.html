Marathi Language Translation Services

Marathi is an Indo-Aryan language and is the official language of Maharashtra, India. It is one of the 23 official languages of India. With over 73 million native speakers as of 2007, Marathi has the fourth largest number of native speakers in India. It is spoken not only in Maharashtra but also in Goa, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, parts of Gujarat, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Dadra Nagar Haveli, and Daman and Diu. Marathi has incorporated many words from Sanskrit and is also spoken by Marathi emigrants worldwide, particularly in the United States, Israel, Mauritius, and Canada.
